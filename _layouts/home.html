About UsIt all started with a place called Smackwater Jacks.

Owner of Smackwater Jacks, Brad Oke saw an opportunity in the Grand Bend area to transport people from some of the many tourist destinations in the area to far apart to walk to. Already running a complimentary shuttle service for the patrons who dined at Smackwater Jacks, Brad used this service to test the interest of local patrons. Over time his idea became a success which lead to the launch of "Tour the Shore" in early July, 2017.


Tour the Shore attracts the idea of becoming a local for the day in the Lambton & Huron County areas, discovering local destinations and supporting local businesses.
